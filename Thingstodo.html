Enjoy the best of Puerto Plata

Epic Places You Must Visit:

Fricolandia

Fricolandia is a unique natural spot that counts with a beautiful beach with gentle waves, private pool, jet ski and play ground for kids. It is surrounded by 3 restaurants and has free parking.Located in Luperon, Puerto Plata. This is totally free but if you would like tours and excursions the price would be $15US and people all ages are welcomed!

Teleferico/Cable Car

The cable car is located in the city, right in the heart of Puerto Plata but Ironically at the Top of a mountain full of Dominican Culture, History and good food. You and your family can be transported through the most beautiful woods to get the most spectacular view towards the Atlantic Ocean. All of this for just $80US, the age range is 12 years-99 years old.

Cayo Arena

Cayo Arena is an Island located in the North of Puerto Plata with the most abundant marine life you can experience face to face. This island is full of corals, damselfish, angelfish, yellowtail snapper, and others. A little powerboat takes people from 10 years and older to Cayo Arena for Only $35 US.
